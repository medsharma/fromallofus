Our Story

It started with
Anika's birthday.

Shivan wanted to do something meaningful for his girlfriend Anika's birthday. So he reached out to her friends scattered across different cities, gathered their messages and photos, and spent days designing a handmade book for her.

When she opened it, she cried. Happy tears. She held it the way people hold things they intend to keep forever.

That moment made something clear: the gift was extraordinary not because of his design skills, but because of the collective love inside it. Everyone who mattered to her, in one place.

He built FromAllOfUs so that anyone — regardless of design experience or free time — could give that same gift. The one that makes someone cry happy tears.
